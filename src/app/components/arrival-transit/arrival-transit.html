Air Travel

Taniti International Airport welcomes daily flights from nearby regional hubs, making it the easiest and fastest way to reach the island. Passengers are treated to stunning views of turquoise waters and lush green hills as they arrive. The airport is modern, efficient, and staffed with friendly locals ready to assist travelers. Taxis, rental cars, and shuttle services are available right outside the terminal, ensuring a smooth transition from the sky to your island adventure.

Cruise Ship Arrivals

Many visitors reach Taniti by sea aboard small to mid-sized cruise ships that dock at the island’s main harbor. The port is located just minutes from the capital, offering easy access to shops, restaurants, and nearby attractions. Disembarking guests can explore the waterfront, join guided tours, or hop on local transportation to see more of the island. Arriving by cruise provides a relaxed and scenic introduction to Taniti’s vibrant culture and natural beauty.
