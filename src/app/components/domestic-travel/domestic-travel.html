Public Transit

Taniti’s public transit system is a convenient and affordable way to explore the island. Modern buses run regularly between major destinations, including the airport, downtown, and popular beaches. With friendly drivers and easy-to-follow routes, public transportation offers a relaxed, scenic way to see more of Taniti without the stress of navigating or parking. Sit back, enjoy the ocean views, and let the island take you where you need to go.

Car Rentals

For visitors who prefer flexibility and freedom, renting a car is one of the best ways to experience Taniti at your own pace. Rental agencies near the airport and city center offer a range of vehicles, from compact cars for city driving to rugged jeeps for off-road adventures. Roads are well-maintained, signage is clear, and driving gives you the freedom to discover hidden beaches, mountain trails, and charming local villages far from the crowds.

Ride Services & Taxis

If you’d rather leave the driving to someone else, Taniti has you covered with taxis and on-demand ride services. Local taxi companies and app-based options make it easy to get around quickly — whether you’re heading to dinner, catching a ferry, or returning to your resort after a day of exploring. It’s a simple, comfortable, and dependable way to travel without worrying about directions or parking.
