Things to Do in Taniti

Escape into adventure, culture, and relaxation. From ocean dives to rainforest hikes, Taniti offers unforgettable experiences for every traveler.

Snorkeling & Diving

Discover the breathtaking underwater world surrounding Taniti. Crystal-clear waters reveal vibrant coral reefs teeming with colorful fish, sea turtles, and other tropical marine life. Whether you’re a first-time snorkeler or an experienced diver, Taniti’s calm bays and guided tours make it easy to explore safely and comfortably. Slip beneath the surface and experience a side of paradise most visitors never see.

Rainforest Hiking

Journey into Taniti’s lush interior and discover trails that wind through tropical forests, waterfalls, and scenic overlooks. The island offers paths for every skill level — from peaceful coastal walks to challenging mountain climbs with rewarding panoramic views. Along the way, you’ll encounter native wildlife, fragrant blooms, and the quiet beauty that makes Taniti so unforgettable.

Local Market

Experience the heart of Taniti’s culture at its lively local markets. Wander through rows of colorful stalls filled with handcrafted goods, island spices, and freshly picked fruit. Friendly vendors welcome you with warm smiles and stories about their traditions. Sampling tropical treats and finding one-of-a-kind souvenirs here is the perfect way to connect with the island’s people and spirit.

Plan Your Day in Taniti

Mix and match activities to make the most of your time on the island. Whether you’re chasing thrills or peaceful escapes, Taniti has something for everyone.
